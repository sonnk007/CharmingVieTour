Top 4 Central Vietnam Amazing Tourist Destinations to Visit in 2024

by Anonymous June 17,2024

Hue

In Vietnam tour packages, Hue is one of the must-visit destinations in Central Vietnam, famous for its cultural and historical heritage such as the Imperial City, royal tombs of the Nguyen emperors, and Thien Mu Pagoda. Hue also features the poetic Perfume River and the ancient Truong Tien Bridge. Visitors can join river cruises on the Perfume River, visit traditional craft villages like the Conical Hat Village and Thanh Tien paper flower village, and savor local specialties like Bun Bo Hue, rice with mussels, Banh Beo, and Banh Nam. In Vietnam travel tours, Hue also hosts cultural festivals like the Hue Festival, royal festivals, and traditional arts like Nha Nhac and Ca Tru.

Da Nang

Da Nang, a dynamic coastal city, is famous for My Khe Beach, one of the world’s most beautiful beaches. The city offers many attractions such as Dragon Bridge, Han River Bridge, Ba Na Hills with its famous Golden Bridge, and Asia Park amusement park. Visitors can engage in activities like surfing, paragliding, and exploring Son Tra Peninsula with its pristine forest and Linh Ung Pagoda. Da Nang’s cuisine is diverse, with specialties like pork rolls, Mi Quang, fish cake noodle soup, and bustling night markets offering delicious street food when exploring Vietnam tourism.

Hoi An

Hoi An, a UNESCO World Heritage site Vietnam provincial tours, is famous for its ancient houses, handicraft shops, and lantern-lit streets at night. Visitors can explore historical sites like the Japanese Covered Bridge, Tan Ky Ancient House, and Hoi An Museum. Activities include cooking classes, releasing lanterns on the river, and savoring street food specialties like Cao Lau, Mi Quang, Banh Bao, and Banh Vac. Don’t miss a visit to Cu Lao Cham, an island near Hoi An, with its clear beaches and rich coral reefs.

Quang Binh

Quang Binh is famous for its magnificent cave system like Phong Nha-Ke Bang, Son Doong Cave, and the beautiful Nhat Le Beach. Visitors can join tours to explore caves, kayak on the Son River, or engage in adventurous activities like trekking and camping in the forest. Quang Binh also has historical sites like Dong Hoi Citadel, Tam Toa Church, and traditional festivals offering a deep cultural experience. Additionally, Quang Binh offers many eco-tourism destinations like Mooc Spring, Tien Spring, and Da Nhay Beach, providing relaxing and interesting experiences for visitors in Vietnam provincial tours
