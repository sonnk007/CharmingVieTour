10 Must-Visit Tourist Destinations in Vietnam for Summer 2024

by Anonymous June 17,2024

Ha Long Bay

Located in Quang Ninh province, Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its thousands of limestone islands and caves. Visitors can explore magnificent caves such as Sung Sot Cave and Thien Cung Cave, engage in activities like kayaking, and scuba diving to discover the rich marine ecosystem, or relax on luxurious cruises to enjoy the stunning scenery at sunrise or sunset.

Sapa

Sapa, a small town in Lao Cai province in northern Vietnam in Vietnam provincial tours, is known for its cool climate year-round and beautiful natural landscapes. Visitors can go trekking to explore vast terraced rice fields in villages like Cat Cat, Ta Phin, and Lao Chai-Ta Van. Climbing Fansipan, the roof of Indochina, by cable car or on foot offers challenging and thrilling experiences. Sapa also has local markets to buy handicrafts and enjoy local ethnic minority cuisine.

Hoi An

Hoi An, situated on the banks of the Thu Bon River, is a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its ancient houses, handicraft shops, and lantern-lit streets at night. Visitors can explore historical sites such as the Japanese Covered Bridge, Tan Ky Ancient House, and Hoi An Museum. Activities include cooking classes, releasing lanterns on the river, and savoring street food specialties like Cao Lau, Mi Quang, Banh Bao, and Banh Vac. Don’t miss a visit to Cu Lao Cham, an island near Hoi An, with its clear beaches and rich coral reefs in Vietnam tourism.

Da Nang

Da Nang, a vibrant and modern city in central Vietnam, Vietnam tourism is famous for My Khe Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. The city offers many attractions such as Dragon Bridge, Han River Bridge, Ba Na Hills with its famous Golden Bridge, and Asia Park amusement park. Visitors can engage in activities like surfing, paragliding, or exploring Son Tra Peninsula with its pristine forest and Linh Ung Pagoda. Da Nang's cuisine is diverse with specialties like pork rolls, Mi Quang, fish cake noodle soup, and bustling Da Nang night markets offering delicious street food.

Ninh Binh

Known as "Ha Long Bay on land," Ninh Binh is famous for its scenic Trang An Landscape Complex, Tam Coc - Bich Dong, and Bai Dinh Pagoda. Trang An is an eco-tourism area with a system of caves and clear rivers, where visitors can take boat rides to explore the stunning natural beauty and visit temples along the way. Tam Coc-Bich Dong is renowned for its vast green rice fields, mysterious caves, and unspoiled landscapes. Bai Dinh Pagoda, the largest pagoda complex in Southeast Asia, features grand architecture, large Buddha statues, and many smaller temples, creating a serene and peaceful atmosphere.

Phu Quoc

Phu Quoc, an island in the Gulf of Thailand in Vietnam tour packages, is a paradise for beach lovers with its clear blue waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious resorts. Visitors can explore famous spots such as Sao Beach, Long Beach, Vinpearl Safari, Vietnam's largest open zoo, and VinWonders Phu Quoc, a large amusement park with many exciting games. Activities include scuba diving, fishing, exploring coral reefs, and enjoying fresh seafood. Don’t miss a visit to Ham Ninh fishing village to experience the local life and try the famous Ham Ninh crab, or visit fish sauce factories, pepper farms, and pearl farms to learn about the island’s specialties.

Mui Ne

In Vietnam tourism, Mui Ne, in Phan Thiet City, Binh Thuan province, is famous for its long sandy beaches, white sand dunes, and red sand dunes. It’s an ideal destination for water sports enthusiasts, offering activities like kite surfing and windsurfing. Visitors can explore Fairy Stream, a small stream flowing through red sand and rock formations, visit Mui Ne fishing village to enjoy fresh seafood, and try sandboarding at the red and white sand dunes for a fun and unforgettable experience.

Da Lat

Da Lat, the city of thousands of flowers on the Lam Vien Plateau in Vietnam provincial tours, is known for its cool climate year-round and picturesque natural landscapes. Key attractions include Valley of Love, Xuan Huong Lake, Cau Dat Tea Hill, and Datanla Waterfall. Da Lat also boasts charming cafes with serene atmospheres and specialties such as grilled rice paper, chicken hotpot with é leaves, and fresh vegetables from nearby farms. Visitors can tour flower gardens, strawberry farms, ancient temples, and churches to experience the city’s tranquility and romance.

Can Tho

Vietnam travel tours to the capital of the Mekong Delta, is famous for the Cai Rang floating market, where visitors can shop on the river and enjoy delta specialties like Hu Tieu, crab noodle soup, and fresh fruits. Ninh Kieu Wharf, a symbol of Can Tho, is perfect for a scenic river walk and visiting the pedestrian bridge. Visitors should also visit fruit gardens like Bang Lang Stork Garden and My Khanh Tourist Village to enjoy fresh fruit and learn about local life. Additionally, Can Tho has several eco-tourism areas where visitors can participate in activities like rowing boats, fishing, and exploring historical sites when exploring Vietnam tourism.

Hue

Hue, the ancient capital of Vietnam, preserves numerous cultural and historical heritage sites with unique architecture like the Imperial City, royal tombs of the Nguyen emperors, and Thien Mu Pagoda. The poetic Perfume River flows through the city, creating a peaceful and romantic scenery. Visitors can also enjoy a rich cuisine with specialties like Bun Bo Hue, rice with mussels, Banh Beo, and Banh Nam. Hue is also famous for its cultural festivals like the Hue Festival, royal festivals, and traditional arts such as Ca Tru and Nha Nhac, bringing unique cultural experiences to Vietnam travel tours.
