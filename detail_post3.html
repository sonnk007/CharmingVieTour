Enjoy 4 Entertainment Services When Visiting Da Nang

by Anonymous June 17,2024

Sun World Ba Na Hills

Experience the world’s longest cable car ride, visit the French village, Le Jardin D'Amour flower garden, and the famous Golden Bridge. The resort also offers numerous exciting games and upscale restaurants with diverse cuisines. At Ba Na Hills, visitors can explore Debay Wine Cellar, and Wax Museum, and enjoy special art performances. The cool climate year-round at Ba Na Hills will provide a pleasant and comfortable experience in Vietnam tourism.

Asia Park

Asia Park is an amusement park with many exciting games, a 115-meter-high Sun Wheel, a diverse cultural area, and entertainment activities suitable for all ages. The park also has restaurants, cafes, and various shopping areas. In Vietnam tour packages, visitors can enjoy thrilling rides, taste special dishes from Asian countries, and participate in cultural events and festivals frequently held at the park. Asia Park will certainly bring you and your family relaxing and fun moments.

Nui Than Tai Hot Springs Park

Relax with natural hot springs, participate in water activities, and enjoy high-end spa and sauna services. The park also entertains such as water slides, wave pools, and children’s areas. Especially, Nui Than Tai Hot Springs Park also offers mud baths and milk baths, providing visitors with great relaxation and health care experiences. The resort area with bungalows and restaurants serving diverse cuisines is another plus for Nui Than Tai in Vietnam provincial tours.

Helio Center

Helio Center is the largest entertainment complex in Da Nang, offering many recreational activities such as a cinema, bowling, a game center, and a children's play area. Visitors can enjoy delicious food at Helio Food & Beverage, participate in regular events and festivals, and shop at fashion and souvenir stores. Helio Center is an ideal destination for families and groups of friends, bringing relaxing and fun moments after exploring Da Nang in Vietnam tourism.
